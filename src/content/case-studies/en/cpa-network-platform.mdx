## Context

The core challenge was not a single marketing website. It was a reusable CPA network platform that could sit behind different brand identities and landing experiences while keeping the frontend maintainable.

In parallel, the same product environment also required an internal CRM with tight messenger integrations for affiliate-facing workflows.

## My Role

- Led frontend development for a team of three engineers
- Participated in task definition, decomposition, and distribution
- Designed frontend architecture for the shared platform layer
- Contributed directly to implementation, including real-time behavior

## Product Scope

- advertiser and affiliate-facing flows
- reporting and analytics surfaces
- API-driven product behavior
- reusable landing and platform patterns
- CRM workflows for communication and funnel movement

## What Changed

- turned frontend work into a more structured delivery stream instead of isolated page work
- designed a frontend foundation that could support multiple branded deployments
- helped shape an MVP CRM for affiliate managers with messenger-oriented workflows
- implemented platform features on a stack built around Nuxt, Pinia, Vite, TypeScript, and WebSockets

## Outcome

The value of this work was architectural clarity and delivery control. The frontend stopped being “just the current landing” and became part of a reusable product platform with clearer ownership, team coordination, and room for growth.
