## Context

The platform had grown through multiple squads and release cycles. Navigation, UI primitives, and content structure were inconsistent.

## What I changed

- Rebuilt page composition around reusable layout rules
- Tightened typography and spacing systems
- Standardized accessibility and interaction patterns

## Outcome

The new system reduced UI drift, improved delivery speed, and created a more stable foundation for future work.
